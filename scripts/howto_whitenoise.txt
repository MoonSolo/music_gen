All customisable parameters at a glance
Parameter		What it does
--typeNoise 		type (white/pink/brown/blue/violet/rain/ocean/fan/womb/binaural)
--variantsHow 		many previews to generate
--previewPreview 	clip length in seconds
--durationFinal 	output duration in seconds
--volumeOutput 		volume 0.0–1.0
--fade_in/outFade 	duration in seconds
--monoForce 		mono instead of stereo
--lowcut/highcutCustom 	frequency filters
--rain_intensityRain 	droplet density
--rain_rumble		Thunder/distant rumble amount
--ocean_wave_speedWave 	cycle speed in Hz
--fan_speed		Fan blade frequency
--womb_depth		Low pulse intensity
--freq_left/right	Binaural beat frequencies
